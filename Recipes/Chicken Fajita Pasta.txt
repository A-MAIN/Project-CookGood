Heat the @oil{3%tablespoons} in a #large pot{} over high heat.
Add the @sliced chicken breasts{3} and cook until no longer pink, ~5{%minutes}, then remove from the #pot.
Add the @sliced red bell pepper{1}, @sliced green bell pepper{1}, @sliced yellow bell pepper{1} and @onion{1} and cook until the onion is translucent, about ~{6%minutes}.
Add the @milk{5%cups} and the @penne{4%cups}, stirring constantly to prevent the @pasta from sticking.
Cook for about ~{20%minutes}, until @pasta is tender and the @milk has reduced to a thick sauce that coats the @pasta.
Add the @cheese{1%cup} and mix until melted, then serve!
